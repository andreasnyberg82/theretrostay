## Troubleshooting & FAQ

### "I'm not getting any bookings"

**Check:**
1. Is your calendar up to date?
2. Is your response rate above 90%?
3. Are your prices competitive? (Check similar properties)
4. Do you have enough photos (10+)?
5. Is your listing complete? (All fields filled)

**Quick fixes:**
- Lower price temporarily to get first reviews
- Add "New Listing Discount"
- Improve your title and first 3 photos
- Turn on Instant Book (Airbnb)

### "I'm getting inquiries but no bookings"

**Check:**
- How fast do you respond? (Under 1 hour = best)
- Is your cancellation policy too strict?
- Are you answering questions fully?

**Response template:**
```
Hi [Name]!

Thank you for your interest in Pod Reglami! I'd be happy to host you.

To answer your questions:
[Answer their specific questions]

The apartment is available for your dates, and I've just sent you a booking offer.

Feel free to ask anything else!
≈Åukasz
```

### "I got a bad review"

1. **Don't panic** ‚Äî one bad review won't ruin you
2. **Respond professionally** ‚Äî shows future guests you care
3. **Learn from it** ‚Äî is there valid feedback?
4. **Bury it** ‚Äî focus on getting more positive reviews

### "How do I handle difficult guests?"

**Before booking:**
- Ask about purpose of trip
- Set clear expectations in house rules
- Trust your instincts ‚Äî you can decline

**During stay:**
- Be available but not intrusive
- Address issues immediately
- Document any problems with photos

**After stay:**
- Be honest in your review (warns other hosts)
- Report serious issues to platform

### "Should I lower my prices?"

**Lower prices when:**
- You're just starting (get reviews!)
- You have long gaps in calendar
- It's low season
- Last-minute availability

**Don't lower prices when:**
- You already have good occupancy
- It's peak season
- Your reviews are excellent
- You'd resent the low rate

### "How do I handle last-minute bookings?"

**Setup:**
- Allow same-day bookings (more bookings)
- Have cleaning service on standby
- Keep basic supplies always stocked

**Tip:** Charge a small premium for last-minute (guests pay it!)
